About Automation Travel Agency

Automation Travel Agency (ATA) provides vehicle booking facilities to users (Customers) across many cities. Our mission is to offer a seamless and efficient travel management experience. Whether you are booking a vehicle for a short trip or a long journey, ATA ensures that you have access to the best vehicles, routes, and drivers.

Our administration interface allows us to add and manage vehicles, routes, drivers, and customers efficiently. Customers can easily book vehicles, cancel bookings, view booking statuses, and access route details through our user-friendly interface.
